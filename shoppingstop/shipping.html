Shipping & Returns Policy

Shipping Policy

We provide fast and secure shipping for all orders across the country.

  • Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
  • Standard delivery takes 3-7 business days.
  • Express shipping is available at checkout.
  • Tracking details will be sent via email.
  • Free shipping on orders above $100.

Return Policy

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it easily.

  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days.
  • Damaged products can be exchanged for free.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
